Contact Us

Customer Support

For product questions and inquiries, please send an e-mail to Customer Support.

Developer Diaries

If you have a question you would like answered during one of our developer diaries, please submit the question to the Lead Developer here. Questions will be chosen amongst the list of those submitted and will be answered at the end of each Developer Diary for our fans.

Fan Art

If you are interested in submitting any fan artwork, please send an e-mail to the Art Team. If you would be willing to permit the art team to use your artwork on the website, please indicate so in the e-mail. We would love to show off your hard work and enthusiasm for our game!

Suggestions

Have a suggestion for the game? Perhaps a new class, race, or monster that you would like to see incorporated into the world? Feel free to send an e-mail to the Development Team. Any constructive feedback of current features are also welcome.
